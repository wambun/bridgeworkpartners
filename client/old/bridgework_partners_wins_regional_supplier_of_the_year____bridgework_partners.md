BridgeWork Partners wins Regional Supplier of the Year! - BridgeWork Partners

November 2011 – The DFW Minority Supplier Development Council won the 2011 National Minority Supplier Development Council Award. Additionally, BridgeWork Partners won Regional Supplier of the Year, with both honors being announced recently at the NMSDC convention, held in Atlanta, Georgia.
