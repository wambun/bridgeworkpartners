Second Annual Callejo-Botello Foundation Leadership Luncheon

![](https://www.powrcdn.com/app_images%2Fresizable%2FScreenshot_0e548270_1767023632605.png)

0


**BridgeWork Partners was honored to support and sponsor the Second Annual Callejo-Botello Foundation Leadership Luncheon, celebrating leadership, education, and civic engagement across North Texas.**

**Our CEO, Wanda Granier, attended alongside inspiring community members and leaders to honor the Foundation’s mission and its ongoing impact. A special thank you to Monica Lira Bravo and Rebecca Acuña for chairing this year’s luncheon and leading such a meaningful event.**

**We also extend heartfelt congratulations to Dr. Elba Garcia, Dallas County Commissioner for District 4, who was recognized for her lifetime of passion, dedication, and impact for Latinas in civic leadership.**

**“Dr. Garcia, you have been an inspiring role model and you are beautiful inside and out. Thank you for also inspiring all of us,” says our CEO, Wanda Granier.**

**The Callejo-Botello Foundation continues to make a difference through:**

**• Scholarships for North Texas students pursuing higher education**

**• Civic education programs that foster informed, connected communities**

**• The Leadership Academy, equipping rising professionals and civic leaders for public service**

**We are proud to stand behind an organization that’s empowering the next generation of leaders and strengthening our community!**
