## BridgeWorkSLED

Through the growth of our company, we are officially adding “BridgeWorkSLED,” to our service offerings. With over 90,000 different government organizations, the State, Local & Education (SLED) market, is incredibly important to the overall landscape of our country and to BridgeWork Partners! We are passionate and have years of experience working with and supporting a number of these organizations, but we are excited to officially add this to our business offerings.
