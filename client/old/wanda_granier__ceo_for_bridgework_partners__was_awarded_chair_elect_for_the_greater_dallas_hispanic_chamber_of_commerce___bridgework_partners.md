Wanda Granier, CEO for BridgeWork Partners, was awarded Chair-Elect for the Greater Dallas Hispanic Chamber of Commerce - BridgeWork Partners

[Open sliding top](https://bridgeworkpartners.com/news/wanda-granier-ceo-bridgework-partners-awarded-chair-elect-greater-dallas-hispanic-chamber-commerce/#)

Wanda Granier, CEO for BridgeWork Partners, was awarded Chair-Elect for the Greater Dallas Hispanic Chamber of Commerce at the recent annual meeting at the Omni Dallas Convention Center Hotel on September 27th. Wanda currently serves on the board and will be installed as Chair-Elect during the first quarter of 2013.

Congratulations Wanda!
